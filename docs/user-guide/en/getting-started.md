# Getting Started

This page explains how to open FutureKawa and find your way around the main
screens. See the [user guide index](index.md) for the full table of contents.

## Accessing the web app

1. Open your web browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox or Safari).
2. Go to the FutureKawa address provided by your administrator.
3. The application opens directly on the **Dashboard**.

The interface works on a computer and adapts to tablets. It is available in a
**light** theme (default) and a **dark** theme — press `t` at any time to switch.

## The main screens

FutureKawa is organised around a left sidebar. Each entry opens one of the main
screens:

| Screen | What you see |
|---|---|
| **Dashboard** | An overview of all countries: key figures, recent alerts, and the health of each warehouse. |
| **Lots** | The full list of coffee lots, sorted oldest-first (FIFO). |
| **Warehouses** (Entrepôts) | The warehouses of the selected country, each with its latest measured conditions. |
| **Alerts** (Alertes) | The list of active alerts (out-of-range conditions or lots that are too old). |
| **Settings** (Paramètres) | Personal preferences, such as the theme. |

## Selecting a country

At the top of the app you can filter the whole view by **country**
(Brazil, Ecuador, Colombia) and, inside a country, by **warehouse**. Your choice
follows you as you move between screens, so you always stay focused on the same
site.

## Finding something quickly

- Press `⌘K` (Mac) or `Ctrl+K` (Windows) to open the **command palette** and search
  for a lot, a warehouse, a page or an action.
- Press `/` to jump straight to search.
